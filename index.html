Yoga


defined as a Hindu spiritual and ascetic discipline, a part of which, including breath control, simple meditation, and the adoption of specific bodily postures, is widely practiced for health and relaxation.

In short, yoga is a type of exercise in which you move your body into various positions in order to become more fit or flexible, to improve your breathing, and to relax your mind.

Slow movements and deep breathing increase blood flow and warm up muscles, while holding a pose can build strength.

Some beginner poses include Downward-Facing Dog, Mountain Pose, Plank Pose, Warrior II, and Tree


Meditation


defined as to engage in contemplation or reflection, is widely practiced for health and relaxation.

Meditation can give you a sense of calm, peace and balance that can benefit both your emotional well-being and your overall health. And these benefits don't end when your meditation session ends. Meditation can help carry you more calmly through your day and may help you manage symptoms of certain medical conditions.
